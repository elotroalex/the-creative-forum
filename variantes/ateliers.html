Ateliers

Each collective research project is an atelier, an opportunity to work together in ephemeral teams, in nimble communities. Each of our ateliers is a chance to learn new tools and methods, to explore new ideas, and to make new friends. We are just getting started, but we have big plans, and the energy to match it. Let us know if you have an idea in mind for a collaborative project in the humanities or are working on one already. We would love to hear from you.
